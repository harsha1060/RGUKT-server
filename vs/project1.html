Welcome to RGUKT RK Valley Event Management

Your one-stop solution for planning, promoting, executing, and receiving feedback on all campus events.

Features

Event Creation and Management

Easily create and manage events with customizable templates, detailed schedules, and calendar integration.

Promotion and Marketing

Automated email campaigns, social media integration, and event reminders to boost engagement.

Registration and Ticketing

Seamless online registration, ticketing options, and waitlist management.

Participation and Engagement

Interactive features like live polls, Q&A sessions, and detailed event agendas.

Feedback and Analytics

Collect post-event feedback and analyze attendance and engagement metrics.

User Access and Roles

Role-based access for students, faculty, and organizers with secure login.
